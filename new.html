About Us & Events

London Bakers and Sweets was established in 1995 with a mission to bring authentic, high-quality baked goods and traditional sweets to the city. We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, following time-honored recipes.

Our master bakers and confectioners have decades of experience creating delightful treats that bring joy to our customers' special moments.
